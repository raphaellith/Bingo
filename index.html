BINGO

How to Play

BINGO is a game that combines prediction, observation and playful mischief. Follow these steps to play:

  1. Customise your board.
    Press EDIT. In each cell of the BINGO board, write down an action or phrase you think your friends are likely to do or say — these could be catchphrases, habits, or funny quirks.
  2. Mark your observations.
    During a party, meal or event, keep an eye out for these actions. Whenever you see one performed, tap on the corresponding cell to mark it.
  3. Aim for BINGO!
    Your objective is to achieve BINGO by marking five cells in a row, column or diagonal. If you want to mix things up, press SHUFFLE. This will randomly rearrange the cells and automatically clear any previously marked cells.

To close this window, press on the info button again.
